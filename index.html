What is GoWorld Youth ¬∑ Âõ¥Ê£ã‰∏ñÁïåÔºü

GoWorld Youth is a youth-led community initiative that uses the ancient strategy game of Go to help younger students practice focus, patience, and good sportsmanship, while offering high-school volunteers a meaningful way to serve and lead.

Our mission

To make Go (Weiqi) accessible to children as a free public good, and to create a safe space where they can think deeply, play fairly, and learn to respect both victory and defeat.

We believe every child should have the chance to experience strategic thinking and quiet concentration in a screen-free, supportive environment.
